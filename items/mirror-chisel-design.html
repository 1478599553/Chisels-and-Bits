--- layout: item category: items permalink: items/mirror-chisel-design/ item: mirrorprint title: Mirror Chisel Design ---

Ever had a detailed design that you wanted to mirror across an axis, possibly a statue or face? Well as you probably already realize rotating that won't help much, but the mirror chisel design will. Just create the pattern on the side that you want to mirror it across and craft it with an appropriate positive, or negative chisel design and your on your way.
