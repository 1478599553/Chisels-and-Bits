Have you make a particular block you really want more of? Of perhaps you simply want to file your design away in a chest in your file cabinet? I'm sure theres other uses but since your here anyway lets talk about the positive chisel design... The positive chisel design represents the positive space of a block, It lets you make exact replicas of your previous designs by apply the design to target blocks in the world, or by crafting it with a bit bag with the necessary materials.
